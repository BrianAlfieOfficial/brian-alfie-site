01 — ABOUT
Live · On Stage

Brian Alfie is an award-winning Filipino singer-songwriter whose voice moves easily between soul-baring acoustic ballads and full-throttle anthems. He placed Top 4 in the Grand Resbakan of Tawag ng Tanghalan, impressing a national panel of judges and putting his name on the map as a serious vocal talent.

Since then he's held down residencies at some of the country's biggest stages — City of Dreams, Okada Manila, and Resorts World Manila — while carrying his sound across borders to Vietnam, Malaysia, and the UAE.

But ask any chapter hall from Cebu to Marikina and they'll know him for one song first: "Triskelion Ako," his anthem for the Tau Gamma Phi brotherhood, now reworked into more than a dozen anniversary versions and sung at gatherings nationwide. It's the thread running through everything he makes — pop, hip-hop-inflected collabs, and the ballads in between.
